Goal:
Turn all the bits black.
Modes:
Immediate
The clicked bit and its adjacent bits get incremented by one.
Continuous
The clicked bit and all bits above, below, left, and right get incremented by one.
Single-Double
The clicked bit gets incremented by one and the immediate adjacent by two. Best played on a 1 row board and more than two colors.
